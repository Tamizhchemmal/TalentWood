Submission Guidline

Originality Matters:

We celebrate originality and creativity. All submissions must be entirely
your own work. Plagiarism or the use of copyrighted material without
permission is not allowed.

Film Length:

To keep the competition engaging and accessible, short films should
not exceed 20 minutes in length. Concise storytelling often leaves a
lasting impact.

Genere Freedom:

We welcome short films of all genres – drama, comedy, animation,
documentary, and more. Let your imagination run wild and choose the
genre that best fits your vision.

Rights and Permissions:

By submitting your film, you grant us the right to showcase it on our
platform and during events. Ensure you have all necessary permissions
for any copyrighted material used in your film.
